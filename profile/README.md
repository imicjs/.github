# Jiangsu Key Laboratory of Intelligent Medical Image Computing (IMIC)

> A provincial-level research platform at [Nanjing University of Information Science and Technology (NUIST)](https://www.nuist.edu.cn/), led by Prof. Jun Xu. We advance AI and information science to solve cutting-edge problems in medicine and healthcare.

## 🔬 Research Areas

| Area | Focus |
|------|-------|
| 🏥 Medical Imaging & Analysis | AI-driven radiology for diagnosis, treatment planning, and monitoring |
| 🧠 Brain Science & Neuroimaging | Computational methods for brain science |
| 🔭 Computational Pathology | Disease diagnosis, prognosis, and treatment planning |
| 🧬 Multi-omics Data Analysis | Integration of genomics, transcriptomics, proteomics, and more |
| 📡 Biosignal & Wearable Devices | Real-time physiological signal capture and analysis |

## 👥 Team

- Interdisciplinary experts from engineering, medicine, and basic sciences
